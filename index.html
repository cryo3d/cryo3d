What is Cryo3D?

Cryo3D is an educational tool, promoting curiosity and exploration. With this goal in mind, the flagship feature of this product is the ability to take predetermined tours around the globe. The program offers “manual” flying, or an “autopilot” mode which automatically takes users from waypoint to waypoint in a flight path. Additionally, users can zoom in and view unique terrains and data layers on the globe. Currently, the addition of extra layers must be done manually in the code, however a UI based addition feature is a future goal.
